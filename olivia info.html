Olivia Isabel Rodrigo

She is an American songwriter, singer, and actress. Rodrigo appears in an Old Navy commercial on screen. Rodrigo appears in “New Girl” alongside Zooey Deschanel as Terrinea in Season Six, Episode 12. She releases her hit single ‘Drivers License’ which shoots to Billboard number one in a week.
