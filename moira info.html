Moira Rachelle Bustamante Dela Torre

A multi-platinum recording artist and powerful storyteller that uses music
and social media to amplify Original Pilipino Music (OPM).
In 2013, Moira Dela Torre was a hopeful talent that turned the seat of Apl.d.ap on The Voice Philippines.
She didn’t win the reality TV show but won the country’s 2017 Himig Handog song writing competition instead. In 2019, she became one of the highly-respected judges in Idol Philippines.
The young artist transformed her career from lending her voice to commercials and
topping the charts of most streamed artists in the Philippines.
Dela Torre’s own albums Malaya and Patawad hit the multi-platinum marks, revealing her own tenacity as a singer-songwriter in the modern age.
It’s unchallenged across the industry that she is reviving OPM and bringing her homegrown talent to the world.
