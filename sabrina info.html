WESTERN ARTIST

Sabrina Annlynn Carpenter

She is an American actress and singer. She is best known for her role as Maya Hart in the Disney Channel spin-off Girl Meets World as well as for her music career. On January 22, 2021, Carpenter released her first proper single in nearly a year with "Skin". This single was her first release after signing with Island Records. This was followed by "skinny dipping" in September 2021. In early 2022, she starred in the films Emergency and Tall Girl 2, and released another single, "Fast Times". On June 30, 2022, Carpenter announced on social media that her fifth album emails i can't send would be released on July 15, 2022. The album's third single, "Vicious", was released the following day. She embarked on the emails i can't send Tour in September 2022 to promote the album. On March 17, 2023, a deluxe edition of the album titled emails i can't send fwd: was released, containing four additional bonus tracks.
